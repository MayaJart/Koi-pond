✿⊰ How to Play ⊱✿

You are a koi fish. Move your mouse and the fish will follow.

  • ✓ Eat smaller dull-colored fish to grow
  • ✗ Avoid larger fish or you’ll be eaten
  • Other koi cannot eat you or be eaten
  • Dull fish may hide or spawn near lily pads
  • Click & hold to dash
  • Press M to mute/unmute music
